Ravi Kumar, @RaviNepal

Digital & Data Strategy Leader. Entrepreneur. Speaker.

Ravi is a data and digital strategy leader, and good governance advocate. He uses technology and media to create opportunities and help deliver aid.

He has been named in Forbes 30 Under 30's list of social entrepreneurs in Asia.

His projects have been featured in the New York Times, NBC, and BuzzFeed.

By day, he leads digital strategy for World Bank's Governance Global Practice, and is helping to build capacity for evidence-based policymaking in Nepal through a World Bank project.

He also volunteers with the non-profit he founded, Code for Nepal, which seeks to increase digital literacy and the use of open data. His TIME op-ed about using data to deliver aid in Nepal was named by the Aspen Institute as one of the five best ideas of the day.

He has also published in the Huffington Post, and World Economic Forum.

He serves on Buena Vista University’s President’s Advisory Council, and was a Penn Kemble Fellow at the National Endowment for Democracy. He often guest lectures and speaks at universities.

In 2009, he co-founded Grassroot Movement in Nepal, a non-profit that has rebuilt more than 25 schools.

He has a Master of Science degree from Columbia University. He has a Bachelor of Arts degree from Buena Vista University. Connect with him @ravinepal.
